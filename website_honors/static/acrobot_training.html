Acrobot Training

Welcome!

This task has two training stages.

You will learn how to swing the bottom of the Acrobot to reach a target line.

Each stage increases difficulty by moving the target higher.

Try to reach the line as quickly as possible!

Time: 0.0 s

Goal
Reach the red line with the bottom joint as fast as possible. Each stage moves the target higher.

Controls
Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to apply torque to the blue middle joint.

Hint
Pump the swing — go with the motion, not against it.
